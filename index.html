7 Principles to Improve Productivity

  1. Set a Daily Main Goal: Start each day by defining one clear primary objective.
  2. Break Down Large Tasks: Divide big projects into smaller, focused subtasks.
  3. Work in Short Sessions: Use the Pomodoro Technique (25 minutes work + 5 minutes break).
  4. Minimize Distractions: Turn off notifications, close unnecessary tabs, and keep your workspace tidy.
  5. Apply the 80/20 Rule: Focus on the few tasks that generate most of the results.
  6. Daily Review: At the end of the day, review what was accomplished and plan for tomorrow.
